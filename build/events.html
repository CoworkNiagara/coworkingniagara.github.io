Events & Activities

Cowork Niagara organizes and hosts events for our members and independent professionals who want to learn from each other about their industries. Whether you’re a writer, developer, designer or other creative entrepreneur, you’ll learn practical tips and tricks you can apply to your business. As a volunteer-based co-operative coworking space, these events can only happen with the continuing support of our members.

Pick an event and come out – we’d love to meet you.

All events are held at Cowork Niagara unless otherwise noted!

Limited Run! Intro to Podcasting

When
October 24, 2015, November 21, 2015, January 23, 2015
Admission
$20 (HST included)
Registration
Eventbrite

Learn how to plan, record and host your podcast. Participants will be able to go from concept to completion.

In one weekend session, get all the information you’ll need to start podcasting. Podcasting is a great way to explore subjects you love with a depth that is sure to connect with others around the world. You can build your business, get better at your hobby, or find other people passionate about your nonprofit’s mission with a podcast.

You’ll be able to use Cowork Niagara’s Podcast Studio to record your first episode (and future episodes, if you’re a member!). Experienced podcasters will walk you through all the steps.
