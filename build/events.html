Events & Activities

Cowork Niagara organizes and hosts events for our members and independent professionals who want to learn from each other about their industries. Whether you’re a writer, developer, designer or other creative entrepreneur, you’ll learn practical tips and tricks you can apply to your business. As a volunteer-based co-operative coworking space, these events can only happen with the continuing support of our members.

Pick an event and come out – we’d love to meet you.

All events are held at Cowork Niagara unless otherwise noted!

OpenHackSTC

Website
Website
When
Fourth Monday of each month from 6:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.
Admission
FREE
RSVP Required?
Yes please!

This event is organized by Software Niagara and hosted in partnership with Cowork Niagara. If you’ve got a web development project to work on or are just looking to get involved, this is the event for you. Hack alone or work with a group on anything you want – all languages, frameworks or licenses welcome. Food is free (usually pizza), but please bring your own (non-alcoholic) drinks. We’ll begin with a round of introductions. OpenHack is open to everyone – members and non-members.
