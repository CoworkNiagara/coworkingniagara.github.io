Visiting Cowork Niagara

Cowork Niagara is located at 108 St. Paul Street in beautiful downtown St. Catharines, Ontario. We're centrally located to many great restaurants and local businesses.

Our space has been beautifully renovated and is extremely spacious. The front of the space has a large group of windows offering an incredible amount of natural light in during the day, as well as an interesting atmosphere during the evening. The main floor is setup with multiple workstations that members can choose from. A bar height multi-user workstation is located at the front of the building, offering a fantastic view of the bustling of the downtown core. Whiteboards allow the visualizing of ideas, and our own Book and Tech Library is a great resource. We have a fully-equipped kitchenette for member use, complete with coffee maker, kettle, toaster oven, and microwave. The main floor is wheelchair accessible as well as an accessible washroom.

The lower level of the space is a large multi-use space, available to all members on a first come, first serve basis. This space is isolatable from the main space above and has 2 washrooms attached to it.

Parking

Affordable and easily accessible parking is available through any of the City-owned and operated parking lots and garages. Parking at certain lots and garages is free outside of normal business hours, Monday through Friday. Be sure to check the hours posted for the location you wish you use.

Metered parking is $1.50 per hour during the hours of 9 am to 6 pm. Parking outside of these hours is free, but parking is prohibited during the hours of 2 am and 6 am. All parking meters accept quarters, loonies and toonies.
