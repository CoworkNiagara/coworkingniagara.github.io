Events for Niagara's Independent workforce

Cowork Niagara proudly offers Niagara's largest calendar of professional development activities for the independent workforce. . Cowork Niagara organizes educational, interactive, fun, free and members-only events and meet-ups for entrepeneurs in the marketing, sales, web and software development industries. We're a co-operative business: Every member is also an owner

Lots of people talk about the importance of community, but we've built it into our DNA.

As Niagara's first coworking community and the only co-operative coworking space in Ontario, we're entirely community owned and managed. Our volunteer board members, directed by our membership, ensure we provide services that directly benefit our members and the community. Find out more about our co-operative and why we chose the co-operative model.
