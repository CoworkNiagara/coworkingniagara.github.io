Five Values. One Community. What Is a Coworking Community? How Cowork Niagara Does Things

  • Meaningful interaction over superficial networking

    We believe that building lasting business relationships, exchanging ideas and spreading knowledge helps us grow, as people and professionals.

  • Starting small over endless planning

    Cowork Niagara started as a grassroots movement that continues to expand. We follow this model as we plan events, activities and milestones.

  • Being done over being perfect

    We celebrate our successes, large and small. We're constantly learning how we can improve as an organization.

  • Collective intelligence over competitive egotism

    Each member brings special talents to the group. We cooperate, not compete, to solve problems.

  • Empowering creative minds over creating a hierarchy

    We continually learn from one another. Everyone's contribution is equally important as we work together to reach our goals.
